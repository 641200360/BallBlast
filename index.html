About Ball Blast

Ball Blast is an exciting arcade-style shooting game where your goal is to blast as many colorful balls as possible with your cannon. The game features simple yet addictive gameplay that's easy to learn but challenging to master.

Originally developed as a mobile game, Ball Blast has gained massive popularity worldwide with millions of players enjoying its fast-paced action. Now you can play the web version directly in your browser, no downloads required!

The game tests your reflexes and strategic thinking as you navigate through increasingly difficult levels filled with bouncing balls. Upgrade your cannon, unlock special abilities, and compete for high scores in this endlessly entertaining shooter.

How to Play Ball Blast

Basic Controls

  • Mouse/Touch: Aim your cannon by moving your mouse or finger
  • Click/Tap: Shoot balls with your cannon
  • Spacebar: Use special abilities (when unlocked)

Game Objectives

  • Destroy all balls in each level to progress
  • Collect coins to upgrade your cannon
  • Avoid letting balls reach the bottom
  • Complete challenges to unlock special powers
